Powerful Features Built for Sales Leaders

Real-time Collaboration

Multiple users working on the map simultaneously with different levels of permission. Once a manager approves a requested change, the alignment automatically reflects on the map in real time — no manual syncing required.

Automated Reporting

Generate PowerPoint presentations with territory metrics, regional summaries, and account tables. Export Excel reports at territory, region, or area levels with workload index, span of control data, and more.

Powerful Bulk Operations

Move entire lists of zip codes to new territories in seconds. Paste zips, select destination territory, and reassign with a single click.

Multiple Basemap Layers

Switch between Default, Satellite, and Plain basemap styles instantly. Choose the map view that works best for your territory analysis—satellite imagery for geographic context, plain for clean data visualization, or default for a classic map view.

How TerraMaps Works

Connect

Import your accounts, territories, roster, and sales data from spreadsheets or CRM systems. Customize layer names and set up your geography hierarchy - territories, regions, areas - to match your organization.

Build

Design territories with precision and speed. Outline zip codes, make bulk moves with lists, toggle layers on and off for clarity, and watch as multiple team members collaborate in real-time with controlled permissions. Customize every visual detail — border colors, thickness, fill colors, and labels — to create a map that fits your workflow.

Refine

Generate comprehensive reports at any level - territory, region, or area. Export to Excel, create PowerPoint presentations with automated screenshots and metrics tables, or export to ZTT format for downstream systems.
